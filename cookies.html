Cookie Policy

Last updated: 4 June 2026

This policy explains how testnowcancellationsandswaps.co.uk, operated by TestNow UK (a trading name of RDS HUB LTD), uses cookies. It sits alongside our Privacy Notice.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files a website stores in your browser. They let a site remember your choices between visits. We use only a couple of simple cookies — no advertising or cross-site tracking.

The cookies we use

Consent cookie (tn_consent) — remembers whether you agreed to region detection, so we don't ask every visit. Set only after you make a choice. Lasts about 6 months.

Region cookie (tn_region) — remembers your approximate area (for example, "West Midlands") so we can show nearby tests first. Set only if you allow region detection. Lasts about 3 months.

What we don't do

We don't use cookies for advertising, profiling, or tracking you across other websites. We don't store your IP address or precise location. If you decline, the site still works — we simply don't pre-sort tests by your area.

Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings at any time. If you clear our cookies, you'll be asked for your region-detection choice again next time you visit. Declining region detection on our banner stops the region cookie being set.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date shows when it last changed.
