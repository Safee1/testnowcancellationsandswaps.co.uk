📋 How to complete your swap

A swap means you and the other learner exchange your existing tests. By law you each deal with DVSA yourselves — no one can do it for you, and it must be done by phone. Work through these together.

Step 1 — Swap booking references

⚠️ Share ONLY your booking reference here. NEVER share your driving licence number, payment card, address, phone number or email with the other learner — give those to DVSA only.

The other learner's booking reference — read this to DVSA
You won't be asked for their name or contact details — only this reference.

Step 2 — Share your test slot (optional)

Optional — if you share your current test, the other learner sees exactly what they'd swap into. Don't want to? Skip this and go straight to the checks in Step 3.

The slot you would swap into
Check this works for you and your instructor before swapping.

Step 3 — Check you both qualify

Both of you must be able to tick all of these. If either can't, the swap can't go ahead.

Step 4 — Have these in front of you before the call

  • Your own driving licence number
  • Your own booking reference number
  • The payment card you booked with (DVSA will never ask for the full number or security code)
  • Details of any driving tests you've taken before
  • The other learner's booking reference (from Step 1)

Step 5 — Call DVSA (both online at once)

You: online Other learner: offline

DVSA won't complete the swap if either of you fails security, doesn't agree, or has used both changes — or if the other learner doesn't answer or has the wrong number on their booking. Check your booking has your correct phone number first.
