For driving instructors

Learners near you need an instructor for their test.

Learners on TestNow sometimes have a test booked but no instructor (or no car) for the day. When they ask for help, we can pass their details to a local instructor — that could be you.

  • Free to register — no subscription, no monthly fee.
  • Get an instant text and email the moment a learner in your regions needs an instructor.
  • Browse available learner requests in your regions (details anonymised).
  • Pay only when you choose to take a lead — their contact details are then released to you alone.
  • Every learner has explicitly agreed to be contacted by an instructor.
  • Registered instructors get an invite to our instructors-only community group.

Save our number. Lead alerts are texted from 07462 169428 — save it in your phone as TestNow so you know it's us and don't miss a lead. Leads are first come, first served.

We handle your data as set out in our Privacy Notice. By registering you agree that we may text and email you about learner requests in the regions you cover. If you choose to join our instructors-only community group, other members of that group will be able to see your name and mobile number. Registering doesn't commit you to anything — you only pay if you choose to take a lead.
